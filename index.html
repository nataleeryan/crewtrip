The vacation planner that works for (and with) everyone.


Planning a trip among friends or family can be difficult.

Our application will help your group coordinate the perfect trip in a fair and fun way. By gathering the preferences of each member, CrewTrip will then generate travel destinations that work best for everyone.


Our API

CrewTrip's API utilizes our technologies to give you the average of the preferences entered by the group, suggested destinations and the place details, and mutual availabilities.
